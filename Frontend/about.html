Honoring Our Martyrs

Preserving the legacy of those who sacrificed for our future

Our Mission

Why We Remember

In each and every country around the world that had wars and even organizations, all prioritize remembering their fallen heroes with memorial days and stones sculpted with their names. These memorials pay tribute to the bravery and sacrifices of soldiers, ensuring their stories are never forgotten.

Memorials help preserve history by providing future generations with a tangible connection to the past. They remind us of the events and people that shaped our nation. The memorials of our fallen martyrs will eternally educate about what they fought for, the righteous causes they stood for against evil and inhumanity. They are the compass guiding people through the roughest seas during their darkest hours.

The Challenge

The number of martyrs and casualties in war can reach hundreds of thousands or even millions, creating a significant challenge in preserving their data. In our digital age, a simple stone slab with names and dates is no longer sufficient. Each new generation needs real heroes with stories and impacts on society that they can connect with.

Our Solution

Our website serves as a digital memorial, hosting comprehensive data about our martyrs - their stories, good deeds, origins, and the causes they fought for. It includes martyrs from different wars in our beloved Jabal Aamel. The platform also enables supporters to send anonymous donations to martyrs' families through their profiles, ensuring their sacrifice continues to be honored through support for their loved ones.
